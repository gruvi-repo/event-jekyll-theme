Visa Information

Visitors from some countries will need a visa to enter Canada. Official information is provided by the Government of Canada website.

By Air

Vancouver International Airport (YVR) is at 13.7 Km from the conference venue. The commute takes 26-minute by taxi or 30 minutes using the public transportation system.

Canada's second busiest airport, YVR served 24.2 million passengers in 2017. Fifty-six airlines serve YVR, connecting people and businesses to more than 127 non-stop destinations worldwide.
